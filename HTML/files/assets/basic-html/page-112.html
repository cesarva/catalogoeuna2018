The works of Vicente Sáenz can be classified as monumental and

largely unknown. Under the seal of all four public universities

and their coordinating body EDUPUC, six volumes have been

published on Saénz. The first, “In the Footsteps of Vicente

Saenz,” is authored by Gilberto Lopes; the second, “Record 1533:

The incarceration of Vicente Saenz in 1939,” by Mario Oliva, and

the remaining four volumes are authored by Saénz himself:

“Breaking Chains,” “Traitors and Despots in Central America,”

“The Cry of Pain and Other Trials” and “Heroic Spain.”
