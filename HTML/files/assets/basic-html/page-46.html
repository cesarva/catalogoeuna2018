Algunos fundamentos de

investigación social

Autores:

Maynor Antonio Mora Alvarado

Juan Rafael Gómez Torres

El texto que usted tiene en lasmanos es una descripción sucintay

clarasobre la investigaciónenciencias sociales desde el punto

de vista de la denominada planificación investigativa, esto es

por el proceso que el investigador social tiene que enfrentar

previo al desarrollo y la búsqueda de conclusiones o de

soluciones estratégicas a problemáticas ligadas con contextos

de malestar y desigualdad social. Referente a la primera fase

de toda la investigación social, abarcando tres modelos que se

han planteado hasta el día de hoy: A) la investigación clásica

o positiva, B) la investigación enfocada en la búsqueda de

soluciones a problemas sociales, C) la investigación acción

participativa o IAP.

The text you have in your hands is a concise and clear description

of the research process in social sciences from the point of view

of the so-called research planning, that is, the social process the

researcher has to face prior to the development and the search for

conclusions or strategic solutions to problems linked with contexts

of discomfort and social inequality. Concerning the first phase of

the whole social research, the work covers three models that have

been raised until today: A) the classical or positive research, that

is to say, the one with which the social sciences appeared or were

born during the nineteenth and early twentieth century, interested

in the explanatory or descriptive knowledge of social facts. B)

The research that is focused on the search for solutions to social

problems, guided by the paradigm of the so-called social and

environmental sustainability.
