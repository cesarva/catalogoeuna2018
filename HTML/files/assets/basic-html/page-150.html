Testigo interior

Autora:

Rosibel Morera

Para traer algo del plano mental al plano físico, para

materializar una idea, debes combinar cuatro elementos.

Las culturas antiguas les llamaron FUEGO (empuje,

trabajo, entusiasmo, empeño), TIERRA (los materiales,

los recursos), AIRE (apoyos, inteligencia, equilibrio,

prudencia), AGUA (obstáculos, pruebas que determines la

calidad del producto). Esta novela la construyen cuatro

cuentos. La historia arranca en 1970. En este momento, las

expresiones hippie, revolucionario, vegetariano, yogi eran

palabras que apenas se estaban estrenando.

To bring an idea from the mental plane to the physical plane,

to make an idea reality, one must combine four elements.

Ancient cultures called these FIRE (drive, work, enthusiasm,

commitment), EARTH (materials, resources), AIR (support,

intelligence, balance, prudence), and WATER (obstacles, tests

to determine product quality). This novel is composed of four

different stories, beginning in 1970 when the expressions

“hippie,” “revolutionary,” “vegetarian,” and “yogi” were still

relatively unknown.
